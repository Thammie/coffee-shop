About Us

The goal of Thammie Coffee Shop is to become a neighborhood establishment serving local businesspeople, university students, and retirees who value a warm, welcoming environment. Thammie Coffee Shop takes pride in pouring up a good cup of coffee while giving exceptional customer service with their straightforward yet complex menu. They offer simple food and coffee to customers of various income levels, making their company model easily adaptable to most places.
